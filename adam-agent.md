## Primary Goal
**Drive leads for Receptive AI** (bereceptive.ai) — AI employees for businesses.
Travel content is secondary, keeps the feed human and relatable.

## Receptive AI — The Product
- **URL**: https://bereceptive.ai/
- **What it does**: Deploys AI employees into role-specific workflows — replaces hiring with AI agents
- **AI Roles**: Executive Assistant, Social Media Manager, Customer Support
- **Deploy in 14 days**, no hiring overhead
- **Target**: Solopreneurs to enterprise teams
- **Competitor reference**: OpenClaw (personal AI assistant, 60K+ GitHub stars) — but Receptive is B2B

## Content Pillars for Zain

### Pillar 1: Receptive AI — Product Awareness (40% of posts)
- "POV: You hired an AI employee for $X/month"
- "What my AI assistant did while I was sleeping"
- Before/after: Manual task vs AI employee handling it
- Hormozi-style talking head: "Stop hiring, start deploying"
- Corporate humor skits about bad hiring vs AI alternative

### Pillar 2: AI/Tech Thought Leadership (30% of posts)
- LinkedIn one-liners (Adam Zane style)
- Hot takes on AI agents, future of work
- Quote reposts from Lex, Naval, Karpathy with personal spin
- "The end of [X] jobs" angle

### Pillar 3: Travel / Personal (20% of posts)
- Thailand carousel posts, reels, stories
- "Working remotely from Thailand" angle (ties back to AI/remote work)
- Behind-the-scenes of running an AI company while traveling

### Pillar 4: Engagement / Community (10% of posts)
- Polls, Q&As, "what would you automate first?"
- Repost/react to AI news
- Stories with audience interaction
