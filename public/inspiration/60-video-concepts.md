# 60 Video Concepts — Zain x Receptive AI

> **Cast:** 3 dudes. No girls. Mix of 2-person and 3-person skits.
> **AI is never on screen** — it's a laptop screen, phone notification, or Slack message they react to.
> **Tone:** @mytechceo meets @corporatedudes meets @fentifriedchicken — deadpan, escalating absurdity, punchy punchlines.

**Cast Key:**
- **A** = Boss / CEO / Main character
- **B** = Cofounder / Senior guy / Partner
- **C** = Intern / New hire / Junior / Client

---

## SECTION 1: AI AGENT SKITS (1–20)
*Directly tied to Receptive AI — AI employees, agents replacing humans, the absurdity of managing AI coworkers.*

---

### 1. "The AI Has a Better LinkedIn Than Me"
**Cast:** A + B | **Style:** @mytechceo

A shows B the AI's LinkedIn profile. It has 50K followers and a "Top Voice" badge. A has 1,900.

> **A:** How does it have more followers than me?
> **B:** It posts 14 times a day. You post once a month and it's always a sunset.
> **A:** That was a STRATEGIC sunset.
> **B:** It got 3 likes. Your mom, your aunt, and the AI.
